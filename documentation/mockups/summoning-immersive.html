For Scene 1: The Overgrown Road, you want an early combat that teaches the party the Witherwild is dangerous without overwhelming them. I'd suggest two options:

Option A: A Vine Horror (Bruiser, Difficulty 3) backed by a pair of Witherwild Crawlers (Minions, Difficulty 1 each). This gives you a front-line threat that hits hard while the minions create flanking pressure. Good balance of tactics and action economy.

Option B: A Thornback Stalker (Skulk, Difficulty 3) with ambush abilities. More of a hit-and-run encounter — creepy, tense, but potentially frustrating if the party can't pin it down early.
