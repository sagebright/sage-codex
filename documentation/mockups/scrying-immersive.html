The Merchant's Ledger Scene 1
Trigger: A player describes searching the overturned cart carefully
Benefit: Find a wax-sealed ledger with merchant routes and contacts
Complication: The ledger also contains coded messages suggesting the merchant was smuggling something
Hollow Bark Cache Scene 1
Trigger: A player examines the unusually thick tree trunks
Benefit: Discover a Wickling supply cache with herbs and a crude map
Complication: Taking from the cache may mark them as thieves to the Wicklings
The Breathing Canopy Scene 1
Trigger: The party rests or lingers in the clearing for more than a few minutes
Benefit: The overgrowth shifts around them, opening a shortcut that shaves hours off their travel
Complication: The shortcut leads through a region where the air itself is thick with spores — Constitution saves or hallucinations
The Forager's Bargain Scene 1
Trigger: A player attempts to gather food or herbs from the forest edge
Benefit: A young Wickling forager appears and offers to trade knowledge for a story from outside the forest
Complication: The forager is being watched by elder Wicklings who disapprove of contact with outsiders
Whispers at the Ruin Scene 1
Trigger: A player approaches the moss-covered shrine and speaks aloud — a prayer, a question, anything
Benefit: A spectral guardian responds, offering cryptic guidance about the Reaping Eye's location
Complication: The guardian demands a memory as payment — the player must describe something their character wants to forget
Shared Scars Scene 1
Trigger: A player shows battle wounds to the dying Havenite soldier pinned beneath wreckage
Benefit: The soldier drops his guard entirely, sharing what he knows about the Havenite command camp and their true reason for invading Fanewick
Complication: His final request is a burden — he begs the player to deliver a blood-stained letter to his daughter in Haven, a city the party may never visit
Resonance Bloom Scene 1
Trigger: A spellcaster uses any nature or growth-adjacent magic within the Witherwild
Benefit: The spell is amplified — treat it as one tier higher, with vivid visual flourishes as the forest responds
Complication: The amplification draws the attention of Nikta's displaced consciousness, manifesting as an oppressive awareness that watches the caster for the rest of the scene
The Cartographer's Mark Scene 1
Trigger: A player studies the crude map from the Hollow Bark Cache or any other Wickling artifact
Benefit: They notice a symbol that matches one on their own equipment — a connection to a larger mystery beyond this adventure
Complication: The symbol is a ward. Tracing it with a finger activates a faint magical beacon that something distant and old begins tracking
